Chapter 6 — The Bridge Chapter

Neural Networks & Deep Learning Fundamentals

The foundation under every CNN, every Transformer, every modern AI system. Strip away the buzzwords and learn how artificial neurons actually compute, learn, and make decisions — one weight at a time.

10 sub-chapters 14 interactive demos 4–5 hours total Foundational

Chapters 4 and 5 introduced two of the most important deep-learning architectures — CNNs for vision and LLMs for language. This chapter is the essential foundation that sits underneath both. If you ever wondered what a "weight," "gradient," or "epoch" really means — this is your structural drawing.

Think of it like reinforced concrete: you can admire a finished bridge, but to design one you must first understand rebar, stress diagrams, and load paths. Neural networks are the rebar of AI.
